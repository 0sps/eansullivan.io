This site was made back in 2015.

 I didn't even think I'd use it for much when I originally made it, but I found out I could post school assignments and other interesting things here. That's when I started School Links, the place where people can see what the homework is for their class (and copy the answers).

But as I found out after doing that for two school years, it isn't easy. I forgot to update it a lot, and when I did it took a long time and seemed like I put in more than I got out of this website. Because of that, I'm not bringing back School Links this year. And since that's one of the only reasons for this site, I'll be shutting it down entirely at the end of this year.

Thanks for everyone who used the site and found it useful. I'm happy people got something out of this as I really tried hard to keep it up. But it's time to stop. Very few people will have the same classes as me, so my assignment calendar wouldn't be very helpful to begin with, and when I consider the work involved I think it's going to be useless.

It was a good run. Hope to see ya soon.

 

-Sean S
